# ORDL Submission Template

Back to publication portal: [README.md](README.md)

Use this template when drafting new ORDL publication documents.

## Title

Provide a concise technical title.

## Summary

State the core contribution and intended audience in two to four sentences.

## Problem Statement

Define the problem and why it matters.

## Methods

Describe methods, systems, datasets, and assumptions.

## Results

Report outcomes, observations, and relevant metrics.

## Limitations

List known constraints, risks, and open questions.

## Future Work

Describe concrete next steps and expected impact.

## References

List internal and external references used.
