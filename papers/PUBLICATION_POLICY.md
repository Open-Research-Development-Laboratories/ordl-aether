# ORDL Publication Policy

Back to publication portal: [README.md](README.md)

## Publication Standards

1. Every publication must identify scope, date, and intended audience.
2. Claims should be grounded in technical evidence, measurements, or cited sources.
3. Architectural assertions should reference implementation artifacts when possible.
4. Limit marketing language and keep statements testable.
5. Include limitations and future work for major papers.

## Versioning and Change Control

1. Major revisions should be logged in commit history with a clear rationale.
2. Publication filenames should remain stable after release.
3. Deprecated papers should remain accessible with archival labels.

## Research Integrity

1. Distinguish internal findings from external references.
2. Distinguish validated results from exploratory hypotheses.
3. Avoid overstating capability without benchmark evidence.
